About
BINI, a Filipino pop sensation with 8 talented members— Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena—has quickly risen to become the top female Filipino artist and second overall in the Philippines. They simultaneously held the #1 and #2 spots on Filipino music charts with "Salamin, Salamin" and "Pantropiko." With over 30M streams and a sold-out "BINIverse" tour, BINI was named one of Teen Vogue’s 2024 Girl Groups to Watch.
